About Me

Samuel CS Yeung

Analyst, Developer, Gamer

Born and raised in Hong Kong, Samuel CS Yeung is a highly detail-oriented data enthusiast with extensive experience in data science and business intelligence. He strives to solve complex challenges and deliver business insights by incorporating skills from mathematics, computer science and business.

Prior to entering college, while Samuel was a top performer at school, he was unsure about his future and career. During his undergraduate study at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, he accidentally realized and found out how enjoyable it was to work with data. This passionately pushed him to explore further and eventually step into the field of data science.

On top of his superb academic performance at college, he accumulated different experiences outside the classroom. A highlight would be the time he worked at the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (a financial regulator in Hong Kong), where he successfully debugged and improved 7 problematic and hard-coded VBA programs, that were used to analyze operational risk data, without any supervision.

Samuel completed his bachelor's degree in 2020 with First Class Honor, as well as the best (i.e., No. 1) academic performer in the cohort. While 2020 was also the year the COVID-19 pandemic started ravaging the world and the job market on an unprecedented scale, Samuel would carry on with his passion and determination in building a rewarding career in data science - the show must go on.
